Our Company


Our company was first founded in 1978. ​It began with a small group of people who loved chocolate, however, they didn’t want to enjoy the blissfulness of chocolate themselves - they wanted to share the chocolate, so that others could enjoy it too. Thus, Choc o’ lat was born. ​Our products are 100% natural, meaning that our ingredients aren't artificial. Our products are also handmade for better quality and flavour.

Our Values


Here at choc o’ lat, we believe that every child should have an equal chance at life. ​However, this isn’t the case in poorer, more corrupt countries. So to help make a difference, we donate 3% of every single product bought from our store and the money goes directly to the Child Poverty Action Group (CPAG). Now you can enjoy our delicious products while knowing you’ve made a difference.
